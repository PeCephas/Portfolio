Case Studies

Selected work

Six examples of how I've diagnosed business problems and delivered measurable outcomes across global teams and platforms.

Platform Implementation IG Group · UK Division
Monday.com Implementation — £97,600 Cost Avoidance
The Problem

The UK Marketing division needed to implement Monday.com across 4 teams. The default path was to engage external consultants at significant cost, with no guarantee of tailored support for IG's operational context.

What I Did

I proactively reached out to the Head of Martech and volunteered to lead the implementation outside my allocated APAC & ME responsibilities. I onboarded 4 teams, designed operational processes from scratch, and resolved a complex Trello data migration after initial tools proved ineffective.

The Outcome

£97,600 in consultancy cost avoidance.

Teams received tailored support from someone with direct knowledge of IG's operational context. Implementation delivered on time with no disruption to workflows.

AI & Reporting IG Group · APAC & ME Division
AI-Powered Interactive Reporting Dashboard
The Problem

Divisional leadership across 4 markets (Australia, Japan, Singapore, UAE) was receiving monthly reports exceeding 100 slides. Heads of Department had to scroll through extensive decks to find information relevant to their market, leading to disengagement from the data.

What I Did

I independently identified the problem, researched AI tools including Claude and ChatGPT, and designed an interactive dashboard that consolidated reporting into a visually digestible, exploratory format. I built a working solution and presented it to my manager, who championed it to senior leadership.

The Outcome

Managing Director and Heads of Department now actively engage with divisional data.

The dashboard replaced passive consumption with interactive exploration, transforming how leadership across 4 markets makes operational decisions.

Process Automation IG Group · APAC & ME Division
Workfront Fusion Automation — 240+ Hours Saved Annually
The Problem

APAC & ME operations teams were spending significant time on repetitive manual processing tasks within Workfront, reducing capacity for higher-value strategic work.

What I Did

I taught myself Workfront Fusion development and built automated workflows that eliminated the manual processing steps entirely. This removed the need for external developer resource while delivering a scalable, maintainable automation solution.

The Outcome

240+ hours of manual processing eliminated annually.

Teams redirected that capacity toward strategic work. External developer costs avoided through self-directed capability building.

Implementation Support IG Group · Europe Division
ClickUp Implementation Support — Europe Division Turnaround
The Problem

The European marketing team's ClickUp implementation was at risk. The project manager had no prior platform experience, and the rollout across multiple teams was falling behind with unresolved requirements and pain points.

What I Did

I stepped in to provide critical implementation support, diagnosing requirements, resolving pain points, and advising the project manager throughout. I built team workspaces and provided ongoing platform support to ensure teams could operate effectively on the new tool.

The Outcome

Implementation delivered on time across multiple teams in Europe.

Teams established consistent workflows in a new platform during a period of significant organisational change.

Workflow Redesign IG Group · Global
Post-Agency Integration — Workfront Workflow Redesign
The Problem

Following the IG Agency's integration into divisional structures, existing Workfront request queues and workflows were no longer fit for purpose. Redundant queues were causing misdirected requests, confusion, and inefficiency across all divisions.

What I Did

I led the end-to-end redesign of Workfront's request queues, workflows, and Taxonomy. I built customised processes for each division from scratch, conducting stakeholder interviews to understand pain points and ensuring data integrity and consistent capture standards across all teams.

The Outcome

Marketing teams across all divisions now operate autonomously.

Misdirected requests eliminated. Faster response times. More agile campaign execution across the business.

Governance & Operations IG Group · Global Agency
Demand Management & Capacity Governance System
The Problem

The IG Agency was receiving more requests per month than it had capacity to deliver — creating over-commitment, missed deadlines, and friction between the Agency and its divisional stakeholders.

What I Did

Working with the operational team, I implemented a quota-based approval process in Workfront — building a demand management system with automated tracking reports that enforced monthly request limits per job type across all divisions.

The Outcome

Transparent, manageable demand across all divisions.

Stakeholders understood capacity constraints clearly. Agency teams delivered more efficiently with realistic workloads and reduced over-commitment.
